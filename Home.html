Our Vision

To achieve our vision, Viveha Trust is committed to sustainable rural development, environmental conservation, agricultural growth, women's empowerment, child development, and fostering community unity for collective prosperity and well-being.

Our Mission

Our mission is to foster sustainable rural development, promote a green environment, support agricultural growth, empower women, nurture child development, and build a united community.

About the Organization

Founded in 2017 by J. Packiaraj Anto Charlee and friends, Viveha Trust is a non-profit dedicated to helping communities and protecting the environment. Without seeking profit or advertising, the trust focuses on several important activities:

    Teaching farmers better ways to grow crops without harming the environment
    Giving scholarships and school supplies to kids who need them.
    Providing food and health check-ups for older people who may need extra help.
    Helping people with physical challenges get the support and tools they need.
    Teaching skills and helping women start their own businesses.
    Promoting activities that keep our planet clean and green.
    Viveha Trust works hard to make life better for everyone and to make sure our Earth stays healthy for future generations.

About us

J. Packiaraj Anto Charlee, founder of Viveha Trust in 2017, is a former community leader deeply involved in social activities, including COVID-19 relief, improving agricultural practices for coconut farmers, promoting environmental sustainability through reduced plastic use and electric vehicles, advising on hygiene, and educating children on healthy eating habits.
